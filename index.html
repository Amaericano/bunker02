Hotel Sultan Cave Suites

Situated on the top of Aydınlı Hill, this unique hotel offers rock-cut rooms with antique furniture from all over Turkey. The hotel's sun terrace offers beautiful panoramic views of the Goreme National Park.

Most of the rooms at Sultan Cave Suites are carved into the cliff for a cool and relaxing stay. All rooms include a seating area and a modern bathroom. Most have a private terrace or balcony.

Cave Suites Sultan has a gourmet restaurant serving Cappadocia specialties. After dinner, on the terrace, you can enjoy a drink and enjoy the sunset over the village.
